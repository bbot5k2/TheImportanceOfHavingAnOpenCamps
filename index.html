The Importance of Having an Open Campus

A Public Service Announcement

Message

Having an open campus can improve student satisfaction and overall school experience.

Evidence

  • Surveys ask students about happiness, stress, and school experience.
  • Schools with open campuses report higher student satisfaction.
  • In a 2021 California survey, 78% of students said they felt more satisfied when given open-campus privileges.

Rhetorical Strategies

Ethos: This PSA uses real student survey data and comparisons between open and closed campuses.

Pathos: Open campuses reduce stress and increase happiness for students.

Logos: The evidence clearly shows that open-campus schools have higher student satisfaction.

Allow open campus lunch for students.
