The Importance of Having an Open Campus

A Public Service Announcement

My Topic

The importance of having an open campus.

My Message

Having an open campus can increase student satisfaction with school.

Evidence That Supports My Message

Surveys often ask students about their happiness, stress levels, and overall school experience. Schools with open campuses report higher student satisfaction scores compared to closed-campus schools. In a 2021 survey of high school students in California, 78% of students with open campuses reported feeling more independent and satisfied with their school experience.

Ethos, Pathos, and Logos

Ethos: This PSA uses real student survey data and comparisons between open and closed campuses.

Pathos: Open campuses can reduce stress and increase student happiness.

Logos: Evidence shows that schools with open campuses consistently have higher student satisfaction.

Have open campuses for students during lunch hours.
