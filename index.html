The Importance of an Open Campus

A modern PSA for Woodcreek High School

Our Message

Having an open campus can greatly improve student satisfaction, reduce stress, and make school a better environment.

Evidence

• Surveys show students at open-campus schools report higher happiness and independence.

• A 2021 study found that 78% of California high school students felt more satisfied when given open-campus lunch privileges.

• Open campuses help students decompress, socialize, and return to class with better focus.

Credibility (Ethos)

This PSA is based on real survey data, student reports, and comparisons between open and closed campuses in California high schools.

Emotional Appeal (Pathos)

Open campus helps reduce stress, boosts happiness, and gives teens the independence they need to thrive.

Logical Appeal (Logos)

The evidence is clear: schools with open campus policies consistently show higher student satisfaction and improved well-being.

Allow open campus lunch hours for Woodcreek High School students.
